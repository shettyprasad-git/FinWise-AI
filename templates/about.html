The Project

What is FinWise AI?

FinWise AI is a free, open-access digital financial literacy assistant designed to help everyday people — students, first-time earners, small business owners, and households — make informed financial decisions without needing a finance degree.

The platform combines conversational AI, practical calculation tools, and curated educational content in one cohesive, easy-to-use web application.

By integrating IBM watsonx Orchestrate at its core, FinWise AI delivers contextual, trustworthy answers — not random internet advice — helping users build financial confidence one conversation at a time.

Our Mission

To bridge the financial literacy gap in India and beyond by making professional-grade financial guidance accessible, free, and available in plain language 24/7.

Technology

Powered by IBM

FinWise AI is built on IBM's industry-leading AI and cloud technologies — the same platforms trusted by Fortune 500 companies worldwide.

IBM watsonx Orchestrate
AI Automation Platform

IBM watsonx Orchestrate is an AI-powered automation platform that lets organisations build intelligent assistants capable of reasoning across tasks, accessing external data sources, and executing multi-step workflows.

In FinWise AI, watsonx Orchestrate powers the conversational chat interface — understanding natural language queries and delivering accurate, contextual financial guidance.

Natural Language Processing Multi-step Reasoning Domain-specific Knowledge Enterprise Security
IBM Cloud Lite
Cloud Infrastructure

IBM Cloud Lite provides a free tier of IBM's enterprise cloud platform, giving developers and students access to compute, storage, AI services, and over 40 managed services at no cost.

FinWise AI is deployable on IBM Cloud Lite using the IBM Cloud Foundry runtime or containerised with IBM Code Engine — ensuring high availability and enterprise-grade reliability.

IBM Granite
Foundation Model

IBM Granite is IBM's family of open, enterprise-grade foundation models trained on curated, enterprise-safe data. Granite models are optimised for business tasks including summarisation, classification, and question answering.

The chat interface in FinWise AI uses ibm/granite-13b-chat-v2 — a conversational variant fine-tuned for helpful, safe, and accurate dialogue. Granite is fully transparent, auditable, and indemnified by IBM.

Open Source Enterprise Safe IBM Indemnified Multilingual
Flask + Bootstrap 5
Web Framework & UI

The frontend is built with Bootstrap 5.3 for a responsive, mobile-first layout and IBM Plex Sans typography to match IBM's visual identity guidelines.

The backend uses Python Flask — a lightweight, production-ready WSGI framework — providing RESTful API endpoints for chat, budget analysis, and EMI calculation, all independently testable and extensible.

This means over 73% of Indians — hundreds of millions of people — lack the basic knowledge needed to manage money effectively, avoid scams, or plan for retirement. FinWise AI exists to change that, one conversation at a time.
